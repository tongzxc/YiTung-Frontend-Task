    Why do I want to be part of this bootcamp? (part 1 of 2)

    I was exposed to the technology field when I was in my final year of my undergraduate studies. It was a collaborative project between NUS and various organisations. I had to work with 4 of my peers to solve a real life problem provided by the organisation we were attached to. It brand new experience for me as this project required coding but I had no prior knowledge in python. However, I actually enjoyed the process of gaining knowledge from scratch and successfully run the script. I love the sense of satisfaction that I get when I succeed in something, especially if I doubted myself initially.


    For my final year project, my thesis was on promoting women leaders in the STEM industries. While gathering information, I managed to interview 3 respectable women who are leaders and/or founders in the technology field. I was so inspired by their dedication to their work and the female community and I aspire to be like them where I can influence and be a role model for younger females. Even till date, I have also joined other female tech community groups and I have supported and attended some of their activities and courses.

    Why do I want to be part of this bootcamp? (part 2 of 2)

    After graduation, I worked in a logistics industry under a graduate program but I quickly realised that it's not the industry for me. In general, I prefer doing more hands on activities such as coding, building a website or analysing data. It was something that my previous job could not offer. I decided to change my career and venture into the technology field by taking up courses in python first as I had some experience. However, I realised that in order to secure a job in the technology field, I need to show my profiency and capability since I have no technical experience. As I am still relatively new to the technology industry, I wanted to try out different aspects of it and narrow my options from there. Through this bootcamp, I hope to gain some technical skills to build up my portfolio, as well as connections from the industry to kickstart my career.


    I hope that through my hard work and dedication, I will be able to eventually secure a job in the technology field and doing something that I am passionate about. Once I am proficient enough, I also hope to mentor and inspire future generations of females! 😊
